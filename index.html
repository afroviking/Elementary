MTRLOG is an electronic driver's journal designed to pack in as many features as possible, in a user friendly manner and with a user friendly price tag. It enables users to log all their journeys without ever having to worry about whether they have pressed any buttons or turned on the app. Its a fully automatic digital gps driver's journal. In fact, it's so simple that the MTRLOG hardware doesn't even have any buttons! You just place the MTRLOG driver's journal device in your vehicle and drive.
